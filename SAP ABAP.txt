The programming language of SAP
ABAP stands for Advanced Business Application Programming.

*Why ABAP is used
To customize SAP as per company needs
To create reports, forms, interfaces, and enhancements
To add logic where standard SAP is not enough

*What you can do with ABAP
Reports (ALV reports)
Forms (SmartForms, Adobe Forms)
Interfaces (connect SAP with other systems)
Enhancements (user exits, BAdIs)
Data conversions (LSMW, programs)
